About Us

What Is mHealth?

mHealth.ai is an initiative to spread awareness about the importance of staying fit and healthy. We are a team of young, energetic and experienced professional doctors, researchers, nutritionists and psychologists, who aim to build a healthier and happier society. Our invite-only community is meant to help and guide people towards better health. Our one-stop platform can assist you to live a healthier life whether you are undergoing any medical treatment or just seeking assistance online.

How mHealth Can Help You?

mHealth.ai, our invite only community based platform, serves as a meeting point for people who have questions about various physical and mental issues, for themselves or their loved ones. It gives them an opportunity to discuss their problems and find possible solutions for the same. Many medical solutions do not prove to be equally effective for everyone. Our expert guidance is just one click away and can provide alternate solutions by discussing their problems.

Mental health, a common problem in people, is normally neglected in our society. Conditions such as depression and anxiety are often dismissed as temporary stress related issues. At mHealth, we aim to help people undergoing these problems to have interaction with our experts in a confidential and one on one basis manner.

How to join mHealth?

To join our community, you should head over to the community section on our website. There you will find the button reading “Join Our Community”. Clicking on which will lead you to a Google form. We need some information regarding you that will help us know you better. Post that, you will receive an email from us, inviting you to our community. Note that we may only select relevant people. The final decision would be of mHealth.
